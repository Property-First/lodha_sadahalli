About us

Lodha Sadahalli

Introducing Lodha Sadahalli – an expansive 70-acre luxury estate crafted to deliver more than just a home, but a complete lifestyle experience. Inspired by timeless European design and surrounded by immersive greenery, this exclusive development blends neo-classical architecture with resort-style living. Set amidst a low AQI environment and overlooking the serene 18-acre Navaratna Agrahara Lake, Lodha Sadahalli offers a holistic, nature-connected lifestyle. With curated amenities, 24x7 concierge and valet services, and thoughtfully designed spaces, every element is built to elevate everyday living. Strategically located in North Bangalore, just minutes from the airport and close to key infrastructure, this marquee project brings together connectivity, exclusivity, and tranquility — redefining the benchmark of modern luxury living.
